The Unwrap Project

Rethinking recycling. Reducing plastic. Restoring balance.

We Grew Up With the Wrong Recycling Idea

Our health and our bay are in danger. Plastic is toxic.

Every piece of plastic ever produced is still here — in landfills, in the ocean, in the air we breathe, the water we drink, and even in the food we eat.

So Let’s Act and Reduce Our Plastic Consumption

Our goal is to ease the transition to plastic-free living in the greater LA area. We offer sustainable alternatives and partner with brands ready to make a difference.

We keep it easy, clean, and accessible. Our first step is Refill@Home — a customizable box of locally made products in refillable containers.

Our ultimate goal is to open a physical store in LA for customers who care about reducing plastic waste.

Our Partners

We curate suppliers who offer both great products and commitment to sustainable bulk packaging.

Our partners meet 4 key criteria:
🌱 Sustainable materials & ingredients
⭐ High quality products
♻ Plastic-free or bulk supplied
📍 Local-first sourcing
